About me

Lanzarote, Spain

Welcome! My name is María García Iglesias. I am an elementary Spanish teacher in Teaneck, New Jersey, where I have been teaching Spanish since 2018. As part of my ongoing commitment to improving my teaching practice, I am currently completing my Master of Arts in the Teaching of Languages (MATL) with an emphasis in Spanish through the University of Southern Mississippi. This portfolio showcases my growth, learning, and reflective practice throughout the MATL program. It includes examples of my classroom work, inquiry-based projects, culturally rich instructional materials, and the ways I integrate second language acquisition research into my teaching. When I am not teaching, I enjoy quilting, reading, and spending time with my family. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy this look into my MATL journey!
